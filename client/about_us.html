The platform Acorn Nursery shall allow BYU-I students/faculty to sign in with their university account to begin working on projects and post new ideas. For a project to be hosted a student must have the required "Acorn's" (currency) amount to sponsor their idea. Once the project is hosted, it shall show up on the platform's job board for other developers to begin development. Acorns to sponsor projects can be earned from the allocated cost that was spent to create a job listing. Acorns shall be distributed across the projects required features for development.
